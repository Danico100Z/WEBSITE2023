Game Idea - Firstly, the game idea was to create a parkour game, but I wanted my game to be a different game, because every parkour game is like the same, so I decided to add something different, then I made a game about evil rats, inspired a bit by the movie ratatouille.
Map - The map idea was to create just a small city in the middle of a island, to make a sense like the rats were invading the island to get revenge by destroiyng this city, and the player mission is to fight against the rats and save the city.
About - This is my first game ever made, so if you notice anything wrong or something which I could have done better, please let me know.
Characters - The characters were inspired a bit in every cartoon rat from different animations, and the boss is a giant rat which is localizated in the middle of the city, the characters from the game were more inspired by the movie ratatouille and the cartoon tom and jerry.
